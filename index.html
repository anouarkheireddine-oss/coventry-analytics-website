FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your audit.

Do we need to do anything technical to get started?
+
No technical knowledge required from your side. We handle everything. On Day 1, you'll share access to your existing Excel files and we take it from there. If you have data in other systems (CRM, ERP, accounting software), we can connect to those too.
Do we need a Microsoft licence for Power BI?
+
Power BI Desktop is free to download and use. For sharing dashboards with your wider team, you'll need Power BI Pro licences (approx. £8.40/user/month). We'll advise you on the most cost-effective setup for your team size during the free audit call.
What if our data is messy or incomplete?
+
Most of our clients come to us with messy data — that's one of the reasons they're switching from Excel. Data cleaning and structuring is included in every package. We'll flag anything that needs your input during the Day 1 audit.
Can we really get this done in 5 working days?
+
Yes — and we guarantee it. We've built a tight, proven process that we follow with every client. The 5-day timeline assumes you're available for a 30-minute kick-off on Day 1 and a review call on Day 4. Beyond that, we need very little from you.
What happens after the project is complete?
+
You own everything — the dashboards, the data model, the documentation. We don't lock you in. Every package includes post-delivery support (30–90 days depending on tier). After that, optional monthly maintenance retainers are available.
What does the free audit involve?
+
A 20-minute video call where we look at your current reporting setup, identify the biggest time drains, and give you an honest assessment of what Power BI could do for your business. There's no sales pressure — if it's not the right fit, we'll tell you.
